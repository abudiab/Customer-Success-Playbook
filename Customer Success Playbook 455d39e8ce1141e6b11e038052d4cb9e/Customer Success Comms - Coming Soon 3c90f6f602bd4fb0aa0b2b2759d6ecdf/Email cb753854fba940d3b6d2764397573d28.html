Customer Check-In and Satisfaction Survey Email

  • Overview

    This can also be called a “Client Satisfaction and Follow-Up” email.

    A "Client Satisfaction Survey Email" serves as a key strategy to engage clients, gather their feedback on your product or service, and understand their needs for continuous improvement and success. It opens up opportunities for further collaboration, strengthening the business relationship.

    When sending a "Follow-Up”/ “Customer Check-In” email to existing customers, it helps you maintain the relationship, check ongoing satisfaction, and identify new needs, emphasizing your commitment to their success and ensuring your offerings remain aligned with their objectives.

Examples:

  • Example 1 (Used AI)

    Subject: Touching Base: How's Everything with XYZ Technologies?

    Hi Joe,

    Hope you’re doing great!
    I’ve been thinking about our last conversation and how we can continue to support XYZ Technologies in hitting those ambitious goals of yours.

    Would you be open to sharing a quick update on how you’re finding our services? I’m particularly interested in:

    1. Any success stories or wins we should celebrate together.
    1. Challenges you’re facing with our solutions.
    1. Ideas or features you think could make your experience better.

    Your feedback is invaluable, not just for steering our partnership but also for shaping the future of our solutions.

    How about we catch up over a call next week? It could be a good opportunity to discuss your feedback in more detail and explore any new avenues where we can be of assistance.

    Thanks for your trust in us, Joe. Looking forward to your insights and continuing to support XYZ Technologies’ journey.

    Kindest regards,

    Mahmoud

    Customer Success Manager - ITG

  • Example 2

    Subject: Friendly check-in: how do you like the newly-added module in the ERP system?

    Hi Bara’,

    I hope everything’s going well at XYZ.

    I wanted to reach out to you to see how you’re feeling about the newly-added module to the ERP. I am hoping you had a streamlined transition without any hiccups! But I wanted to hear from you about it.

    Would you be able to share any feedback about your experience so far?
    Particularly, are you feeling that the new module is helping you cut down on processing times and increase your team’s efficiency and hit those KPIs?
    How satisfied would you say you are with the new implementation?
    Do you have any concerns or comments or even suggested improvements?

    At ITG, we’re always trying to go above and beyond and identify any room for improvement, so I would like to invite you to a meeting to further discuss your experience so far and address your suggestions and thoughts. I’m available next week and the next. Let me know the time that works for you and I’ll do my best to accommodate that!


    I would love to hear back from you on the above at your convenience; Please don’t feel rushed!

    Kindest regards,

    Mahmoud

    Customer Success Manager - ITG
